Consultant Profile

Experience

I began my career in the manufacturing hubs of East Asia and North America, working within the logistics infrastructure of Fortune 500 electronics and retail companies. Over 15 years, I observed a consistent issue: businesses frequently overpay for procurement contracts due to structured information asymmetries and outdated negotiation frameworks.

Consulting Approach

Unlike corporate agencies that send junior analysts to drop generic templates, I operate as an embedded director. I travel directly to suppliers' production floors, analyze pricing architectures from raw materials upwards, and lead active contract negotiations alongside executive teams.
