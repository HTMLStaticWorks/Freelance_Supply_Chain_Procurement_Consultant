Procurement Strategy

This engagement builds robust direct and indirect sourcing models that insulate companies from logistics fluctuations and pricing increases. I evaluate vendor concentration to eliminate single-point-of-failure risks.

Sourcing Process:

Phase 1: Category Mapping

Auditing and categorizing your historic purchasing data to highlight high-spend components.

Phase 2: Supplier Mapping

Identifying fallback global vendors to diversify raw materials sourcing paths.

Supplier Audit Services

I conduct independent audits directly on manufacturing facilities globally. I verify production capacities, examine internal quality inspection steps, check ISO compliance, and evaluate geopolitical exposure.

Audit Process:

Phase 1: QA Evaluation

Validating on-site testing procedures to verify compliance with engineering blueprints.

Phase 2: Capacity Verification

Analyzing factory floor machinery utilization to ensure supplier can meet seasonal demand spikes.

Schedule On-site Supplier Auditing
Book Supplier Audit Consultation

Cost Reduction Analysis

This analysis conducts direct bottom-up costing. I look past supplier catalog pricing to evaluate raw material indices, processing tariffs, and packaging costs, allowing clients to enter negotiations with empirical data.

Analysis Process:

Phase 1: Component Costing

Breaking down the build pricing structure of your products to outline raw material margins.

Phase 2: Negotiation Playbooks

Drafting technical counter-proposals to reduce supplier margins with data indicators.

Initiate Cost Reduction Diagnostics
Book Spend Assessment

Vendor Management

Transitioning key supplier relationships into KPI-driven frameworks. I design service level agreements (SLAs) with built-in penalties and incentive structures that align with delivery performance goals.

Vendor Process:

Phase 1: SLA Modeling

Drafting clear service and quality frameworks to enforce operational expectations.

Phase 2: Performance Tracking

Deploying scorecards that rank supplier performance across quality, cost, and logistics.

Deploy Performance Scorecard Frameworks
Schedule Vendor Assessment

Supply Chain Optimization

I review shipping routes, custom tariffs, freight carrier configurations, and inventory storage buffers. The objective is to minimize inventory carrying costs without risking delivery bottlenecks.

Optimization Process:

Phase 1: Inventory Calculations

Using statistical models to define optimal safety stock limits for every regional warehouse.

Phase 2: Freight Carrier Reviews

Restructuring carrier RFQs to consolidate volume discounts and reduce transport rates.
